My Hobbies


My name is Amy and this is what I like to do in my spare time.

Knitting and Crochet

My granny taught to me to knit and crochet when I was little and I have been knitting and crocheting (with varying degrees of success) ever since. Knit fast, die warm!

Reading

A wise sage once said that "reading books and buying books are two entirely separate hobbies" and I am definitely partial to both. I will read anything, but I particularly love science fiction, cosy crime, romance and historical fiction.

Baking

I absolutely loved baking with my mum growing up and baking those childhood favourites makes me so happy... although if I am being entirely honest, my hobby probably falls less into the category of baking and more into that of eating baked goods.
